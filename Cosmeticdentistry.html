Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr. Ramla Ahmed offers a variety of cosmetic dentistry to help you achieve the perfect smile. We have the talented dental professionals to give you all the cosmetic treatments you need at our office; we can help you if you simply want whiter, brighter teeth or if you want to completely redesign your smile.

There are many dental treatments that fall under the umbrella of cosmetic dentistry. All of these treatments are meant to enhance the beauty of your smile in order to have a more beautiful and inviting smile that boosts your self-esteem and helps you smile with confidence.

We may suggest cosmetic dentistry for any of our patients who would like to improve the beauty of their smile. Cosmetic dentistry might be the solution you need if your smile is flawed by imperfections such as:

  • Cracked, chipped, fractured or broken teeth
  • Gaps and spaces between teeth
  • Discolored, yellowed or stained teeth
  • Slightly misaligned or uneven teeth
  • One or more missing teeth
  • Teeth that appear too long, too short or too pointed
  • Very worn teeth
  • Misshapen teeth

Our dental office offers a range of cosmetic dental treatments that can help you reach the smile of your dreams. Your individualized treatment plan will ensure that your needs and desires are met, and we look forward to talking with you today to learn more about what we can do to improve your smile’s appearance. We’re excited to be a part of the process and help you get the smile you deserve!

Cosmetic Offerings

Composite Dental Fillings

Composite fillings, or tooth-colored fillings, are used to repair cavities, chips, and fractures. They are made of a synthetic resin blended with a microhybrid composite powder — similar to an epoxy. The resin can be applied as a paste or in liquid form and then burnished to a smooth finish.

Learn more about composite dental fillings​.

Dental Bonding

Dental bonding is a cosmetic dental treatment used to repair chips, close gaps, or change the shape and color of your teeth. Unlike other cosmetic treatments such as porcelain veneers, dental bonding is completely reversible (and affordable).

Learn more about dental bonding.

Porcelain Dental Veneers

Dental veneers are wafer-thin, custom-made shells of tooth-colored materials designed to cover the front surface of teeth to improve your appearance. These shells are bonded to the front of the teeth changing their color, shape, size, or length.

Learn more about porcelain dental veneers​.

Porcelain Dental Crowns And Onlays

Dental crowns are caps placed on top of damaged teeth. Crowns are used to protect, cover and restore the shape of your teeth when fillings don’t solve the problem. Dental crowns can be made out of metals, porcelain, resin and ceramics. They typically don’t require special care over time other than regular good oral hygiene.

An onlay is used when a cavity is too large for a regular filling. They are pre-formed in a laboratory before being bonded to the decayed or damaged tooth. They are a more conservative approach than a dental crown since they do not involve the removal of healthy tooth structure.

Learn more about porcelain dental crowns​ and onlays.

Teeth Whitening

There are many teeth whitening systems and products, including whitening toothpastes, over-the-counter gels, rinses, strips, trays, and whitening products you get from a dentist. Teeth whitening is ideal for people who have healthy, unrestored teeth (no fillings) and gums. Individuals with yellow tones to their teeth respond best, but this cosmetic procedure is not recommended for everyone.

Learn more about teeth whitening.
